Gorilla

Gorillas are ground-dwelling, predominantly herbivorous apes that inhabit the forests of central Africa

  • Scientific Name: Gorilla gorilla graueri
  • Average height: 5.2 - 5.6ft
  • Average Lifespan: 35-40 years
  • Habitat: African Tropics

Western gorillas are smaller than their eastern counterparts and have shorter hair. The gorilla, one of the four great apes, is the planet's largest primate. They are found in the cloud and rain forests of Central Africa.
